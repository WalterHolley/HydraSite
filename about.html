About


The objective of Slay Hydra is to provide an engaging environment in which students can learn about coin-toss probability. The player will take the role of an ancient Greek warrior who must battle the mythical Hydra, a monster with multiple deadly heads and tails which can grow back after being cut off. The player will try to outwit Hydra by learning the probability of winning, given a sequence of moves. A dodge to the left or right will represent the coin toss or heads or tails.


Can you outsmart the Hydra? Play the game to test your fate!
